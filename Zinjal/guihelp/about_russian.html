(ZinjaI is not just another IDE)


    ZinjaI is a C++ IDE (integrated development environment) initially developed in the context of a Scholarship for Initiation to the Scientific Research, in the Universidad Nacional del Litoral, specially designed for being employed in classrooms by programming studends.

    If you found a bug or have questions or suggestions you can write to zaskar_84@yahoo.com.ar. or download the latest release from zinjai.sourceforge.net.

    To read a full list of changes and improvements made on every release, go to Change Log. To obtain information about the package licenses and its distribution go to Licenses And Contacts.
